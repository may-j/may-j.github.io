Women In STEM

At WISTEM, we plan trips to big STEM companies, invite speakers to speak with us, and work with younger kids to help them get a jump in STEM. We believe that everyone should have an equal oppurtunity to succeed, and our mission is to empower women to have the confidence to take risks and challenge stereotypes.
