Speech And Debate

Speech and Debate is an extracurricular activity designed to build communication skills through public speaking competition. MVLA Speech and debate brings together students from both of our district high schools to train and and practice as one team. Membership is open to all high school students in the Mountain View Los Altos Union High School district. There are no tryouts; anyone can join. All you need is an interest in acting, arguing, reading, researching and/or writing, and a willingness to give tournaments a try.
